Homemade Bread

From the start of the whole process, keeping ingredients together, the wet and dry ones, to mixing the dough is an interesting experience. But what whips up the feeling of excitement and joy is watching the reaction of the whole family. The moment the dough is in the oven, it take about 20-25 minutes for the aroma to permeate the home, and even the outside. The kids are all filled with excitement to gorge the oven fresh Homemade bread. It gives me joy to watch all of them, suddenly competing for who will be the first to taste the bread. That happens to be the small reward of moms who can spare a little time to provide home made meals for their families.

I certainly haven't travelled because of baking bread. But I have had cause to travel to Pennsylvania and Clinton Village to see my kids who were schooling both in Hamilton and BrynMawr Colleges. On those occasions, I had to relive the experience for them, bake bread, scones and cake, and watch them still react as kids on the sight of the oven baked fresh bread.

Learning CSS

I have learnt how to create a css folder right from the desktop, and save it into the html folder am working with. But i noticed that the things am styling in the css sheet is not showing on my webpage. So, I asked Emem what the issue was, and he showed me, I did not link it properly. When the arrow is facing my right, it shows that the style.css file is not really in the css folder, but when the arrow is facing down, and the style.css is indended, it is properly done.
