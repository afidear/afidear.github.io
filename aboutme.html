Homemade Bread

From the start of the whole process, keeping ingredients together, the wet and dry ones, to mixing the dough is an interesting experience. But what whips up the feeling of excitement and joy is watching the reaction of the whole family. The moment the dough is in the oven, it take about 20-25 minutes for the aroma to permeate the home, and the surrounding. The kids are all filled with excitement to gorge the oven fresh Homemade bread. It gives me joy to watch all of them, suddenly competing for who will be the first to taste the bread. That happens to be the small reward of moms who can spare a little time to provide home made meals for their families.
